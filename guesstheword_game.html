Finance Concept:Insurance

Insurance has a great importance to the economy. It increases individual welfare by increasing security.There are two broad type of insurance. 1. General Insurance: General is a insurance based on a contract that offers financial compensation on any loss other than death. Most common general insurance are; car insurance, house insurance, fire insurance etc. 2. Life Insurance: Life Insurance is a contract that offers financial compensation in case of death or disability. Widely used life insurance is Whole Life Insurance and Term Insurance. Whole Life Insurance covers for lifetime while Term Life Insurance covers for only specific period.
